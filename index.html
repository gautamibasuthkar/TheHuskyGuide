About Us

Learn More About Us

We are a diverse group of former international students and local experts passionate about cultural exchange and supporting newcomers in our community.

  • International Student Guide is dedicated to helping international students navigate their new surroundings and find a taste of home.
  • We aim to connect students with local resources, cultural experiences, and a supportive community.
  • Eat. Shop. Connect

We hope this website becomes a useful resource in the Northeastern University, Oakland Community.
